Sweep the Earth

Sweep the Earth is a venture that was born from one of the core principles that we live by at Verdad Tech — innovation. At the end of 2014, a friend and I were brainstorming ideas that we could work on together. We eventually came up with a web app much like StartupQuote.com, but instead of startup quotes, we did photo quotes related to the LDS Community. Verdad Tech allows us to innovate on ideas such as Sweep the Earth. Read more about how it all started.
